Louise

Client

Rachel redesigned my garden two years ago now and everyday I still wake up, look out and love it. I had quite a specific idea about what I was looking to achieve and Rachel was very good at listening, taking on board my ideas and reinterpreting them into a cohesive garden design. Rachel’s sourcing of hard material samples is extensive and she works with you to choose the right stone. Having gone through this extensive process, for us, the right decision was to stick with what we all ready had. Rachel successfully, along with her brilliant landscaper, moved pavers to create entertaining space in other parts of the garden. When it comes to choosing plants Rachel has extensive knowledge and having listened to your colour and basic knowledge requests produces an extensive list of options for you to look at and choose from. Unfortunately for us, our garden was being planted as COVID restrictions hit and things ground to a halt for two weeks. Rachel was professional throughout this time. She kept in contact with me, advised me on watering and caring for the plants and even popped over when she legally could. Her landscapers came back as soon as they could and the project was finished without any more hiccups. I would highly recommend Rachel, she is a down to earth designer who listens and understands and holds your hand throughout the whole process. Too many garden designers feel they know best and find it difficult to listen to their client. Rachel certainly is not one of these.
