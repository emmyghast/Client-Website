Urban Chic Garden

The owners of this Balham garden were keen on making the most of their space to be used for entertaining and relaxing. We put in raised beds with inbuilt seating, and replaced the paving with a durable sandstone and put cedar cladding for the fencing. We have replanted their olive and tree fern and introduced a mix of evergreen osmanthus topiary for all year round interest with a mix of ferns, grasses, climbers and perennials to ensure all year round interest ! We also installed lighting so it can be enjoyed after dark and from the kitchen on an evening. The clients are delighted with their new garden.
