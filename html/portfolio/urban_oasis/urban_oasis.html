Urban Oasis Garden

Home to a family of four, this small town garden was in need of a total change in its layout. We put in some new raised beds with informal seating where they can dine and relax. We clad some of the walls with a lovely cedar and installed contemporary porcelain tiles for low maintenance. Lighting has been added so that the garden can be enjoyed after dark and give a lovely view from the kitchen at night. The planting was rejuvenated with a mix of evergreen topiary and shrubs, climbers and herbaceous perennials and grasses to give all year round interest.
